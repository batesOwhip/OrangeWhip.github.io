More than just a sport...

Bates Ultimate Frisbee is a community dedicated to creating a fun and inclusive environment that can meet any competitive demands. Whether you are looking for some light exercise with your friends, or to push yourself to your peak athletic performance, Orange Whip has what you are looking for.

Come one, come all!

Whether you are a seasoned vet or never have thrown a frisbee before in your life, the fall semester is all about developing fundamentals and getting to know the team. During a normal year, we have mutliple tournaments which are a great way to improve your skills and build your confidence on the field.
